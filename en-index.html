About Helyos

A human-scale firm, deep expertise

Helyos is a consulting and training firm founded in Tunis in 2021, specializing in human capital development. We work with organizations of all sizes across Tunisia and the MENA region.

Our approach combines analytical rigor with close proximity to teams. Each collaboration is an opportunity to exchange, learn and build lasting solutions together.

What guides us

Our values

Integrity

We say what we do, and we do what we say. Our recommendations only have value if they are honest, even when they are uncomfortable.

Rigor

Every intervention is prepared, structured and evaluated. We refuse approximation, whether in program design, trainer selection or results measurement.

Transmission

We don't deliver content, we accompany people. What matters to us is what remains after the training: a new skill, a different posture, an enhanced capability.
